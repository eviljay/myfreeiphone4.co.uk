4 Things You Need to Know about the Upcoming Pokemon Games

October 2, 2016 / admin / 0 Comments

Nintendo is home to the Pokemon franchise and their upcoming games will be available on the Nintendo 3DS portable game console.

The new Pokemon games are called “Sun and Moon” and much like its predecessors, both of them have the same plot but with different Pokemon and enemies.

The game will be released soon so in this article, I will talk about the 4 things you need to know about the upcoming Pokemon Sun and Moon games:

1. Starting Pokemon: If you’ve played Pokemon games before, you know that when you start out, you will be able to choose your starting Pokemon. That trend continues with the Pokemon Sun and Moon and the starting Pokemon you can choose from are Litten, Popplio, and Rowlet. These Pokemon represent three different elements: Fire, Grass, and Water. It doesn’t matter which Pokemon you will choose first as they will be good enough as a starting Pokemon.

2. You Can Ride a Pokemon. This feature was introduced back in the Pokemon X and Y. Basically, there are some particular Pokemon that you can ride, although not all Pokemon can be ridden. This is a perfect way to bond with your Pokemon and it would be a fantastic experience once you ride one of your Pokemon and traverse through the city.

3. New Pokemon. Just like every iteration of Pokemon games, there are always going to be some new Pokemon to choose. Although it hasn’t been fully disclosed, Nintendo teased the fans and they’ve showcased some of the confirmed new Pokemon that will be available in the game. Some of the new Pokemon are, Drampa, Cutiefly, Charjabug, Tapu Koko, Togedemaru, and Bruxish, and Vikavolt. Unfortunately, there still aren’t any pictures of the new Pokemon online. So I guess we will have to wait until the game comes out to see them all.

4. Different Gameplay Elements. Much like Pokemon games of old, the Pokemon Sun and Moon will have the same plot. But, there will be some major differences in the game. The different versions will have a different set of Pokemon and that the trainers that you will face will also have a slight variation as to what Pokemon they will use. Furthermore, there will be a few different Pokemon that will be available in the sun version and there will also be a few different Pokemon that will be available on the Moon version as well.

So, the question now is, when will the Pokemon Sun and Moon be released? I am glad you asked because the Pokemon Sun and Moon will be released November 23, 2016.

There is also news that whenever you pre-order the game, you will also receive limited edition Pokemon merchandise for free.

Fans of the Pokemon franchise would be happy as the new Pokemon Sun and Moon games are available for the Nintendo 3DS portable game console.

So Pokemon fans, mark your calendars and save the date, November 23, 2016!
