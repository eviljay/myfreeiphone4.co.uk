Before we discuss the D-Link DIR-813 AC750 Wi-Fi Router even further, let’s take a look at its features:

  • Next Generation Wireless Technology – Wireless AC750 for optimized performance and reliable coverage delivering smooth HD video streaming, fast file transfers and lag-free video chatting.
  • Dual Band Performance – Up to 300Mbps (2.4GHz) + 433Mbps (5GHz) to deliver fast wireless speeds and less interference for maximum throughput
  • Wired Connectivity – Four Fast Ethernet ports for fast device connectivity.
  • High-Performance Antennas: 3 high-performance antennas deliver maximum range around your home.

The DIR-813 uses a smart WiFi technology because it can separate gaming and multimedia streams for uninterrupted play. Really old routers do not have this technology, and games and multimedia are shared in one stream. This results into laggy gameplay and stuttering when streaming videos.

But, with the D-Link DIR-813, that is non-existent. Gaming streams will be put on the 2.4GHz band, while streaming and other multimedia services will be put on a much faster 5GHz band.

Moreover, the DIR-813 boats of an impressive range. According to their website, the WiFi signals can penetrate even thick walls. Enjoy gaming and streaming anywhere on your home and office, that is the promise of the DIR-813.

Also, the DIR-813 uses the “Fast Ethernet” technology, where it boasts of speeds of up to 10x faster than the conventional Ethernet port. This means that you will get impressive speeds whatever you do online, such as gaming or streaming media content.
